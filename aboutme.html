About Me

My name is Michael; thanks for visiting my website! I am a cognitive neuroscientist interested in human behaviour, statistics, and data modelling. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Oxford, where I also completed my DPhil (PhD) on work intersecting cognitive psychology and neurobiology. I previously worked in the National Health Service (UK) in data analysis and clinical-facing roles.

My work has been featured in the media, including The Mirror, The I, MedicalXPress and the University of Oxford frontpage.

I created this website mainly as I wanted to improve my skills with CSS and JavaScript. However, it was also a means to consolidate my skills, experience and publications (and to have a home for my photos as well!).

If you would like to know more about my skills and experience, please see my C.V. section.

Research

I am always on the lookout for research opportunities. If you would like to work with me or need some advice on a piece of work where my skills align, please reach out. You can reach me at my current academic email — michael.colwell@psych.ox.ac.uk.
